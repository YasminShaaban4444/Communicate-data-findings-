Airline Delay Causes Dataset¶

by Yasmin Shaaban¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the number of flights within years 2019 and 2020 compare them to see the effect of COVID and the causes of delays that could be used to influence number of flights, which carrier that is used more . The main causes are weather , security and National Aviation system.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consisted of attributes of approximately 30803 of flights . The attributes included the number of flights delayed to weather , Natianal Aviation System , security also total number of flights arrived , as well as additional measurements such as airport name and carrier name. 2225 data points were removed from the analysis due to inconsistencies or missing information.
